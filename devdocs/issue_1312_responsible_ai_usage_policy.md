# Responsible AI Usage Policy for StoryBuilder Foundation

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping creative practices and workflows at an unprecedented pace, but we at StoryBuilder Foundation believe that human storytelling and human insight remain at the heart of artistic creation. This policy outlines the principles and guidelines for using AI in association with our tools such as  StoryCAD and StoryCAD Collaborator. Our goal is ensuring that AI is used to **assist** rather than replace the human creative process.

> **Note:** This document uses the Jasper.ai Responsible AI Usage Policy template as a starting point, and is adapted to our specific needs. (Source: :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0})

## Core Principles

1. **Creator Ownership:**  
   The creator of a story or outline retains complete ownership of their work, including all intellectual property rights. Users are encouraged to copyright their works. Control over access to one’s prose or outline remains solely with the creator.

2. **Human-Centric Creativity:**  
   StoryBuilder Foundation’s AI tools are designed to assist and enhance your creative process—not to generate complete, publishable works of fiction autonomously. Our tools focus strictly on developing the structure and “bones” of a story in the form of outlines, and do not provide an end-to-end creative solution like some writing applications.

3. **Transparency and Ethical Use:**  
   When AI is used to assist with outlining or other aspects of the creative process, its role must be clearly transparent. Any AI-generated suggestions or structural enhancements should be acknowledged as such. We commit to using AI in an ethical manner, consistent with our respect for human creators.

4. **Compliance with Applicable Laws:**  
   StoryBuilder Foundation adheres to all applicable laws and regulations. We expect our users to maintain best practices regarding data privacy, intellectual property, and security in any AI-powered work environment.

## Guidelines for Responsible AI Usage

### Transparency
- We are committed to being transparent about our AI-assisted tools. All enhancements and suggestions generated by our AI are provided solely as an aid to the human creator.
- **Users must acknowledge** in any public or professional use when AI has contributed to the creative process.

### Ownership and Control
- Users retain full control and ownership of their creative outputs. StoryBuilder Foundation tools do not claim rights over user content.
- We advise all users to protect their creative output (e.g., via copyright registration) to ensure their rights are maintained.

### Appropriate Use of AI Tools
- **Assistance Only:** Our AI services are meant to support creative structuring and idea generation. They are not to be used to fully automate the creation of creative writing.
- **API Usage:** For initial rollouts, users must obtain their own API key from OpenAI (or other approved AI platforms). We are also exploring integrations with Anthropic’s MCP, with clear mechanisms for resource access control.
- **Third-Party Tools:** Any external tools or AI services used in conjunction with StoryBuilder Foundation must be vetted and approved by StoryBuilder. Unapproved tools should not be used for company-related work.

### Content Integrity and Ethical Considerations
- **Authenticity:** Full AI-generated prose is not acceptable. Human judgment must guide the final form of any published or publicly shared work.
- **Bias and Fairness:** AI systems learn from existing data and may reflect biases. We commit to reviewing AI-assisted content for any potential bias, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility remain paramount.
- **Misuse Prevention:** AI should not be used to impersonate other individuals or to fabricate content in a misleading or harmful way.
  
### Usage in a Broader Creative and Immersive Context
- **Focus on Story Structure:** Our commitment is to support writers in developing story outlines and structure. We do not support using AI to generate full works, and this policy extends to all offerings from StoryBuilder Foundation.
- **Future Applications:** As creative AI tools evolve, particularly in immersive storytelling (as discussed in *Hamlet on the Holodeck*), we will update our guidelines to reflect new opportunities and emerging risks while staying true to our core principles.

## Accountability and Updates

- **Responsibility:** Humans remain ultimately responsible for the creative output. Any mistakes or misrepresentations made by AI-assistance must be addressed by the user or company as appropriate.
- **Policy Updates:** As the field of AI-assisted creative work is rapidly evolving, this policy will be periodically reviewed and updated. Users will be informed of any major revisions.

## Acceptance
By using StoryBuilder Foundation AI tools, you agree to adhere to the terms outlined in this policy. Non-compliance may result in restricted access to our services.
